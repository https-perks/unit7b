Cache Artifacts & Official Clues

Only agents with current clearance may access these fragments.
Some caches may remain hidden until the proper phase is reached.

Phase 1: Activation

  • Bear Mail Arrival:
    “The time has come. Your paws have been deemed ready. First contact has appeared in your location’s tote.”
  • Sealed Envelope:
    Contains Unit 7-B lore (tattered, mysterious), unique to each team.

Phase 2: The Hunt Begins

  • Anonymous Clue Packs:
    • Canyon: “You’ve already walked past it. The message was placed under surveillance 27 hours ago. One of your own carries it now.”
    • Lake: “Delivery complete. The mark has been made. The message now rides with your team—look closer than usual.”
    • Mammoth: “The chapel has eyes. A trusted hand within your ranks holds the message now. You’ll know when to ask. Or you won’t—and the trail will go cold.”
    • Old Faithful: “The fire’s story has already passed to your team. Someone you work with holds the next piece. They have been instructed. You have not. Keep it that way.”

Phase 3: Interference

  • Warning: Unit 7-B reports interference. Another team seeks the Cache. Proceed with caution. Message compromised. Next drop point altered.
  • Canyon Evidence:
    • Blurry photo. Caption: “Subject active. Location confirmed. Cache proximity: HIGH.”
  • Lake Message (Raccoons): “This is your only warning. The cache belongs to us now. You were never alone.”
  • Mammoth Evidence: “...found near Map R█o█. Not safe. ████ has eyes. Avoid contact until ██:██ PM. – 7-B(?)”
  • Old Faithful Message: “Too slow. Clue extracted. Maybe next time, Field Agents. — The Raccoons”

Phase 4: Task Rabbit

  • Bear Bureau HQ: “Request verification. Submit one pinecone, one marshmallow, and one haiku.”
  • Instructions: Teams text submissions to HQ number for next code/location.

Phase 5: The Lost Pages

  • Artifact: Weathered, burnt-edge page from the Field Manual—may contain cipher key, map fragment, or riddle.
  • Reference: Hints to the final cache location. (One piece always missing.)

Phase 6: Discovery

  • Final Transmission: “This is Unit 7-B. Final location is secure. Bring only the worthy. The cache will be revealed.”
  • Cache Contents:
    • Official Unit 7-B patches or badges
    • “Wildlife Operative Field Manual” (summer summary)
    • Stickers, snacks, “Contraband Ration Type E”
    • Printed group photo marked “TOP SECRET”
    • Final scroll message:
      “They searched for us all summer. But they were never meant to find us...
      They were meant to become us.
      Welcome to Unit 7-B.”
Some Cache Files Redacted
Further caches, QR codes, or intercepted Bear Mail will be unlocked as phases progress.
